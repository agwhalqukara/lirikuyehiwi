Conor Maynard releases music video for &quot;Understand Me&quot;

It’s been sometime since Conor Maynard released an original. Maynard for last few years, has been covering songs by other artists. His “Sing Off” concept has taken the Internet by storm and has been well received by the fans. But fans for a long time, have been waiting for an original by the singer.

Friday turned out to be rain in the desert. Maynard and CMC$ released an original track called “Understand Me”. The duo released the music video via YouTube.

“Understand Me” is an cathartic electric dance music track. The drum sticks slowly takes one to a climatic beat drop that initiates the classic EDM music by CMC$. The harmonizing tone can take you high and take you into a completely different zone.

A love track at its core, Maynard can be seen expressing his feelings for his beloved. As usual, Maynard sounds beautiful. His vocal prowess will definitely send chill down your spine. Check out the video below:
