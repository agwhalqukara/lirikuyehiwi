Megan Thee Stallion and Cardi B Announce New Collab Single Bongos Celeb Secrets

Hot girl summer is NOT over quite yet, as Megan Thee Stallion and Cardi B are coming together to release a new single “Bongos” on Friday (September 8).

The two teased the cover image art for the new song earlier this week, where both ladies are wearing colorful wigs and holding lollipops for their upcoming music.

Cardi and Megan haven’t shared further details yet, but this is their second collab, so the hype is really real.

“Bongos” follows the release of the duo’s “WAP” back in 2020, which broke the Internet thanks to some raunchy lyrics and an iconic music video that has racked up over 16 million views to date.

Since the release of “WAP,” Cardi B and her husband Offset recently released a new song titled “Jealousy,” while Megan Thee Stallion has been keeping a low profile in terms of music for the last year.
