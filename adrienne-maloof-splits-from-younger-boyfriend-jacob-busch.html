Adrienne Maloof Splits From Younger Boyfriend Jacob Busch

Looks like Adrienne Maloof's romance with Anheuser-Busch heir Jacob Busch has lost its buzz. The former Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star, 53, has split from her much-younger beau of a year-and-a-half, 25, sources confirm to Us Weekly.

According to TMZ, which first reported the breakup, the relationship started to fizzle out last fall. Around that time, Maloof returned to RHOBH in a couple of cameos on the current season, bringing Busch along as her date. (She left the show after three seasons in 2013 but came back to "mend" some "unfinished situations," as she told Us last summer.)

Maloof and Busch first stepped out together back in October 2013, when they were spotted making out at an event in Hollywood. Days after that sighting, the reality TV veteran spoke to Extra about their romance, calling him a "lovely person."
