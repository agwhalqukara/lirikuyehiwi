Beyonce's Bridesmaid Dress at Mom Tina Knowles' Wedding Revealed: Pics

Beyonce not only attended her mom’s wedding—she was a bridesmaid, too!

The “XO” singer’s mom, Tina Knowles, tied the knot with Richard Lawson in an elegant yacht wedding on April 12. And now, less than two weeks later, Beyonce has shared intimate portraits from the affair via Tumblr, some of which feature herself in two lovely bridesmaid dresses.

At the center of one group photo, the pop crooner, 33, posed (with her tongue sticking out!) in a form-fitting lace dress with a plunging V-neckline. Adding to the boho feel of the cinched dress, Beyonce wore a crown of flowers on her head. (Later in the night, the Grammy winner changed into a white, off-the-shoulder mini to boogie down with Blue and the other guests.)

Just as glam: Blue Ivy, 3, who modeled a miniature princess dress with a poofed tulle skirt and teamed with satin ballet flats. The mini fashionista wore her short hair in two little buns at the top of her head, which kept a tiara in place.

Both Bey and Blue’s ensembles were in keeping with the entire bridal party, who exclusively wore ivory for Lawson and Knowles’ special day. For Solange, who was also snapped enjoying the ocean breezes with her son, Daniel, 10, and niece, Blue, that meant a one-sleeved, caftan-styled dress. Her husband, Alan Ferguson, looked dapper in well-tailored, light suit.

Likewise, Jay Z chose the classic hue for his own suit, which, as seen in another adorable shot, he unbuttoned to bend down to his daughter’s height so that they could dance together.
