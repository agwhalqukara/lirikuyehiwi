Dizzee Rascal Releases New Single Body Loose - OUT NOW!

British rapper is gearing up for release of 7th studio album.

Fresh from the release of of comeback single L.L.L.L featuring MC Chip, and the explosive Act Like You Know with the Smoke Boys, music pioneer Dizzee Rascal unveils new single Body Loose from his highly anticipated forthcoming album E3 AF?. 

This ginormous track, with it’s irrepressible beat and hypnotic rhymes and rhythms, looks to one of the legendary musical eras of the past, and samples The Architechs classic Body Groove as a tribute to UK Garage. 

Produced by Splurgeboys, the track serves as the latest cut to be lifted off of Dizzie Rascal’s 7th studio album E3 AF – a 10-track layered, purposeful statement of intent, rooted in Dizzee’s indelible ties to both east London and Black British music’s legacy.
