Pokmon Legends Arceus: Where to Find the Pokmon With &quot;Three Leaves&quot;

There are actually a couple of good places to find a Petilil at this point in the game, and they’re both located in Crimson Mirelands. Once you’re in that area, you’re going to want to head over to either Cottonsedge Prairie or Holm of Trials. Alternatively, you can just ride around Lake Valor for a while. There are usually more than a few Petilils hanging around that general area.

On that note, it’s worth pointing out that this quest is slightly easier to complete if you’ve already unlocked Basculegion. It’s not impossible to complete it without a mount, but you’ll certainly make things easier on yourself if you decide to explore this area with a little help from one of the game’s rideable Pokémon.

It shouldn’t take you too long to find a Petilil once you start roaming around those locations, but be sure to sneak up on it when you do spot one so that you don’t accidentally scare it off.

Once you spot a Petilil, you’ll simply need to catch it and eventually give it to the quest giver, but it’s worth noting that you will lose the Petilil you just caught after you’ve completed this quest. If you’re looking to also add a Petilil to your collection, be sure to catch more than one while you’re on the hunt. If you do happen to catch more than one, be sure to keep the more powerful one and then turn in the other to complete the quest.

Aside from any extra Pokémon you may decide to keep as part of this quest, your reward for completing “The Search of Bitter Leaves” will be three Fine Remedies and five Hopo Berries.
