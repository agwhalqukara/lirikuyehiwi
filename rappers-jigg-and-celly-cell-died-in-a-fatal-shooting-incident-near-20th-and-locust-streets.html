Rappers Jigg and Celly Cell Died in a Fatal Shooting Incident Near 20th and Locust Streets

In a devastating incident that has shaken the Milwaukee community, local rappers Jigg and Celly Cell have been identified as the victims of a fatal shooting near 20th and Locust streets. The tragic event unfolded on December 18, 2023, leaving fans and fellow musicians in shock and mourning.

A Shattering Incident

A horrific double shooting incident occurred in a quiet Milwaukee neighborhood, claiming the lives of two beloved local talents – Jigg and Celly Cell. One victim was found dead at the scene, while the other was pronounced dead upon arrival at the hospital, according to officials.

About Jigg and Celly Cell

Both Jigg and Celly Cell were well-known figures in the Milwaukee rap scene, recognized for their unique sounds and contributions to the local music community. Their sudden and violent deaths have left a void in the hearts of many, with tributes pouring in from all corners of the city and beyond.

An Ongoing Investigation

Authorities are currently investigating the circumstances surrounding the fatal shooting. Community members are encouraged to come forward with any information that might aid in the investigation. Meanwhile, the Milwaukee community continues to mourn the loss of these two talented individuals who made significant contributions to the local music scene.
