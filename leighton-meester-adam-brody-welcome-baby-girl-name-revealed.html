Leighton Meester, Adam Brody Welcome Baby Girl, Name Revealed

Xoxo to this baby girl! New mom Leighton Meester and her husband, Adam Brody, have welcomed a baby girl. The gender was revealed on Thursday, Sept. 24, shortly after Us Weekly broke news that the Gossip Girl alum had given birth.

TMZ obtained the child's birth certificate, revealing that the stars named their newborn baby daughter Arlo Day.

According to the document, Meester, 29, and Brody, 35, secretly welcomed their baby girl in a hospital that wasn't the usual celeb-favored Cedars-Sinai Medical Center. The actress gave birth to the little one on Aug. 4.

Meester was last seen publicly this past July in L.A.'s Sherman Oaks neighborhood, where she beamed and cradled her big baby bump.
