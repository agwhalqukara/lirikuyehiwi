PUSHA T AND WIFE VIRGINIA WILLIAMS EXPECTING FIRST CHILD

Congratulations to Pusha T and his wife, Virginia Williams, on the expectancy of their first child! The celebrity couple shared the news of their family’s expansion on Monday (December 9).

“Finally landed my dream job,” Virginia told her Instagram fans. “World, make way for BABY THORNTON! Arriving Gemini season 2020! We are over the moon,” the rapper’s pregnant wife said.

Pusha T also took to social media to celebrate the impending birth of his and Virginia’s little one. The rapper shared a snippet of the lyrics to a song slated for inclusion in his upcoming album to shed light on his growing family. “Life goes on and babies born,” Pusha wrote online. “And mines on the way,” he continued. “Couldn’t wait to say it in song.”
