What is Rosie McClelland famous for? Her Age, Family, Boyfriend

Who is Rosie McClelland?

British social media star, singer, actress and YouTuber Rosie McClelland, was born under the zodiac sign Virgo on 7 September 2006, in Essex, England. She has only four acting credits, and is perhaps known best for playing Patrol Garda in a 2017 episode of the thriller mini-series “Paula”, which starred Siobhan Cullen, Tom Hughes and Denise Gough, and follows a successful chemistry teacher whose life’s become complicated now that he’s had a one-night stand with a mysterious woman; the mini-series won one of its four award nominations.

Education and early life

Rosie was raised in Essex by her mother Danielle McClelland who’s a housewife, and father Greg McClelland whose profession remains undisclosed, but who’s believed to be working at a local supermarket; Rosie has a younger brother Romeo McClelland, and he can be seen featured in a couple of her Instagram pictures and videos.

One can say that Rosie was thrown into the film industry as she was only six, when cast to play Ruby in two episodes of the 2013 family comedy series “Sam & Cat”, which starred Cameron Ocasio, Ariana Grande and Jennette McCurdy, and was created by Dan Schneider. It follows two roommates who’ve launched a baby-sitting business, and the series won three of its 10 award nominations.

Rosie fell in love with acting following this first performance, and was then in 2014 cast to star in the family adventure comedy movie “Sophia Grace & Rosie’s Royal Adventure”, which also starred Margaret Clunie and Sophie Grace, and was directed by Brian Levant; it follows Rosie and her best friend Sophia as they’ve been sent to Switzelvania by “The Ellen DeGeneres Show”.

Rosie’s only other acting credit has been voicing both Grace and Georgie in three episodes of the 2019 short animated comedy series “The JoJo & BowBow Show Show”, which starred Nia Sioux, Case Walker and JoJo Siwa, and follows the adventures of JoJo Siwa and her pet dog; the series was nominated for a 2020 Young Entertainer Award for Best Young Actress – Voice Over Role for Giselle Lomelino.

Rosie hasn’t had any roles since then, focusing on her education. She enrolled at local high school in 2021, and should be matriculating in 2025; she’s today also focused on running her YouTube channel and various social media accounts, which represent her only source of income.

YouTube channel

Rosie launched her self-titled YouTube channel on 8 October 2011 and there are today nearly 1.3 million people subscribed to it, while it numbers over 800 videos uploaded – all of these together number over 260 million views, and most feature her during her everyday life, whether she’s spending time with her best friends, shopping for clothes or travelling. We’ll be covering Rosie’s three most viewed videos as these have contributed to her overall popularity.

Her #1 video “How I got on the Ellen show and met Nicki Minaj! Part 1 #shorts” has been watched over 23 million times since 22 October 2022, and it today numbers nearly 800,000 likes; it features Rosie talking about how she and her friend were invited to appear in the popular talk-show “The Ellen DeGeneres Show”.

Rosie’s second most popular video “Handstand by Rosie McClelland (Official Music Video)” has been watched over 11.5 million times since 26 May 2018 and is her original song.

Her #3 video “OMG My Little Brother Was So Annoyed! #brother #shorts #annoyed” has been viewed over eight million times since it was posted on 20 March 2023, and has amassed nearly 300,000 likes; it features Rosie and her friends dancing, and annoying her brother.

Presence on social media networks

Rosie’s followed by close to a million people on Instagram, and has posted over 2,300 pictures onto the network; most feature her during her everyday life, whether she’s spending time with Sophie, attending various events or singing and dancing.

She’s tweeted more than 4,200 times since the creation of her Twitter account in October 2011, and there are today over 110,000 people following her; most of her tweets are about her dancing and acting career.

She’s quite popular on TikTok as more than 2.5 million people are following her on the network, and all her videos together number over 120 million likes; most feature her lip syncing to her favorite songs and dancing.

Rosie has a Facebook profile, as well, but only uses it to keep in touch with her real life friends and family members.

Love life and relationships

Rosie prefers to keep her love life away from the media’s attention, and is yet to talk about any boys whom she’s perhaps dated; most of her fans believe that she’s too young to be dating, but there are also those who think otherwise.

Interesting facts and hobbies

She’s interested in fashion and keeps up with the latest fashion trends by reading various magazines.

Rosie enjoys spending her spare time outdoors, whether she’s walking around her city with her friends or relaxing at a park close to her home.

One of Rosie’s favorite actresses is Bailee Madison, and a couple of her favorite movies are “Just Go With It”, “Bridge to Terabithia” and “A Week Away”.
