Marcel Ruiz and Isabella Gomez Spill One Day At A Time Season 3 Tea! Celeb Secrets

It’s baaaaack!

One of our favorite comedies on Netflix, One Day At A Time, just dropped THIRTEEN new episodes on the streaming platform that we may or may not have binged already.

Without giving any spoilers, the upcoming season features more comedy, real-life issues, and guest stars including Gloria Estefan, Stephanie Beatriz (Brooklyn Nine-Nine), Melissa Fumero (Brooklyn Nine-Nine), India de Beaufort (Kevin (Probably) Saves The World), and show runner Gloria Calderon Kellett. With the Alvarez kids growing up and facing new issues each day, they see what life looks like, in good times and bad — and how people like Penelope (Justina Machado), Lydia (Rita Moreno), Dr. Berkowitz (Stephen Tobolowsky) and Schneider (Todd Grinnell) make it all worthwhile.

Celeb Secrets sat down with Marcel Ruiz and Isabella Gomez recently to get the scoop on what’s to come from the third season. The young actors touch on the situations each kid will face, how they overcome it, and what they’d like to see their characters do in upcoming episodes.
