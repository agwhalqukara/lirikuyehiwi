Paige DeSorbo Claims 'Winter House' is &quot;Cancelled&quot;

Credit: Instagram

Paige DeSorbo made quite the announcement on her Instagram Story on Monday.

Following reports claiming Winter House has been cancelled after just three seasons on Bravo, the Summer House cast member, 31, told her fans and followers that the show would not be returning as an insider suggested that it may have simply been put on pause.

Along with a photo of a model walking in a purple flowing cape at a fashion week event in New York City, Paige offered a caption, which stated, “Me at Winter House even though it’s cancelled.”

Then, after an Instagram fan account reshared her post and told their online audience, “Winter isn’t coming I guess,” Paige confirmed where she learned of the apparent news.

“Sorry I thought we all read it on [Bravo and Cocktails],” she wrote, as seen in a screenshot provided by Bravo and Cocktails on Instagram.

Bravo and Cocktails also shared an additional Instagram Story in which they responded to a concerned fan who didn’t understand why Bravo would have potentially gotten rid of the series.

“Whatttttttt it’s cancelled how it’s so good,” the person had written in response to Paige’s post.

“Paused for now not def forever,” the fan page replied, also adding in a caption, “It’s not filming this year. Could return.”

Winter House made its debut in 2021 and aired its most recent season, season three, from October 2023 to December 2023 with a full-time cast that included Amanda Batula, 32, Brian Benni, 32, Casey Craig, 31, Jordan Emanuel, 31, Katie Flood, 32, Danielle Olivera, 35, Alex Propson, 29, Tom Schwartz, 40, and Malia White, 33.

Summer House season eight premieres on Thursday, February 22, at 9/8c on Bravo.
