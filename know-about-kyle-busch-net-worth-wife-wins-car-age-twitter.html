Born as Kyle Thomas Busch, age 34 has over 200 racing wins that cement his net worth to be in multi-million figures. But apart from his toiled career, the racer had to face a low life phase after losing his second child in 2018.

American professional racer, Kyle Busch currently races full-time in the Monster Energy NASCAR Cup Series, driving the No. 18 Toyota Camry. The 2009 NASCAR Nationwide Series winner is also the 2015 NASCAR Sprint Cup Series Champion.

Kyle Busch Bio & Family

The racer was born on 2 May 1985 in Las Vegas, Nevada. His parents, Tom and Gaye Busch raised him and his older brother, Kurt Busch, age 40 who is also a well-known stock car racing driver. Kyle stands at a height of 6 feet 1 in (185 cm) and weighs around 84 kg.
