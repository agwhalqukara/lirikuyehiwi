Kat Von D Releases Exorcisms Remix EP

Gifted multi-hyphenate Kat Von D celebrates her exploration of the darker sides of love with the release of her ‘Exorcisms’ remix EP.  The EP features reworks of the haunting single by  British synthwave pioneers  Gunship, darkwave favorite Cold Cave, French evocative synth pop purveyors Scratch Massive, and Kraftwerk’s Henning Schmitz aka NEO. An expanded Deluxe Remix EP with additional reworks will follow on November 30, to coincide with Kat’s global live stream event ‘The Love Made Me Do It Experience!’, in partnership with Moment House.

The remix EP follows Kat Von D’s acclaimed synth-driven debut LP, Love Made Me Do It, released on September 30.  In addition to the synth-laced lead single, ‘Exorcism’, other darkwave bops released include ‘I Am Nothing’, ‘Enough’, and ‘Fear You’. The LP features collaborations with Dave Grohl, Linda Perry, Dave Sitek (TV On The Radio), Peter Murphy (Bauhaus), Danny Lohner (Nine Inch Nails), Ladyhawke and Charo.
