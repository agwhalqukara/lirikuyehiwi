Kendall Jenner Is Babette From Beauty and the Beast at Givenchy: Pics

Be her guest!

Budding supermodel Kendall Jenner walked the runway at the Givenchy Spring/Summer 2015 fashion show in Paris on Friday, June 26—but that’s not the amazing note. (After all, she is one of the world’s most in-demand models!)

Jenner, 19, stomped the runway in a lavender, peekaboo (aka nipple-baring) lace minidress with some serious statement fringe attached at the hemline. As she made her way down the grunge set, she kicked all those tassels into motion so that they swirled around her feet, which made her look a whole lot like a feather duster.

And not just any feather duster…

…but Beauty and the Beast‘s Feather Duster! (Who was known as Fifi or Babette, depending on the adaptation.) As you may recall, she famously romanced the seductive candlestick Lumiere. Look at the skirts on these ladies.
