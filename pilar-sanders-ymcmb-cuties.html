PILAR SANDERS YMCMB CUTIES

Pilar Sanders’ three tweens know how to rock Young Money clothing. The gang was recently photographed wearing YMCMB attire looking cool as ever. “Thanks to the fam over at YMCMB for keeping the kids fly for school,” said Pilar.

Pilar and former husband Deion Sanders are currently in the middle of a very public divorce in which everything from ulterior motives to prenuptial agreements is in question. Although the battle seems to be one for the long haul, Pilar is determined to remain in the game for her children’s sake. “I’m not going to give up for my children. I’m going to continue to fight for what they want and deserve,” explained the former model.

Deion previously agreed to pay $10,500 per month in child support to Pilar, but now claims that such amount is too expensive and has opted to pay the state maximum for each child. The former NFL star gave his wife $100,000 sighing bonus when she agreed to a prenuptial agreement several years ago. Pilar will receive $1 million from the agreement after divorce proceedings are finalized.
