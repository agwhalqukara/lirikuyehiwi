Nina Dobrev News - Us Weekly

Name:Nina Dobrev

Hometown:Toronto, Canada

Birthday:January 9, 1989

About

Nina Dobrev was cast on the Canadian series Degrassi: The Next Generation in 2006, starring as Mia for three seasons. Her star continued to rise with her leading role on The Vampire Diaries opposite Ian Somerhalder and Paul Wesley, but she left the CW show after six seasons in 2015.

Her film credits include The Perks of Being a Wallflower (2012), The Final Girls (2015), Flatliners (2017), XXX: Return of Xander Cage (2017) and Love Hard (2021).

Dobrev dated Somerhalder, her Vampire Diaries love interest, from 2010 to 2013. After brief relationships with Derek Hough, Austin Stowell and Grant Mellon, Dobrev began dating Shaun White in 2020.
